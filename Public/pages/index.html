Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about QuizWhiz+

Is QuizWhiz+ free to use?

+

Yes, QuizWhiz+ offers a free plan with access to most features. We also offer premium plans with additional features for power users.

Can I create my own quizzes?

+

Our intuitive quiz builder makes it easy to create custom quizzes on any topic. You can add multiple-choice questions, true/false questions, and more.

How do I share my quizzes with others?

+

Once you've created a quiz, you can share it via a unique link, embed it on your website, or share it directly on social media platforms.

Can I use QuizWhiz+ for educational purposes?

+

Yes! Many teachers and educational institutions use QuizWhiz+ to create engaging learning experiences. We offer special features for educators, including class management tools.
